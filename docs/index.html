Psycheros 0.10 community addons

Manager-native where current; reproducible where historical.

Local music presence, accessibility controls, continuity connectors, and thread export without presenting old source replacements as current Psycheros addons.

These are community projects, not official Psycheros releases. HTF Music Listener 0.2.0 and Accessibility Controls 0.1.0-rc.1 are the current Psycheros 0.10 plugin-manager packages. Source mods remain versioned history only.

HTF Music Listener 0.2.0

A trusted Psycheros 0.10 plugin for explicit one-off listening and opt-in shared Windows Now Playing presence.

  • Durable local HTF sensory library
